Introduction to Digital Photography Final Project: The Color in the Cords

For my Intro to Digital Photo final project, I wanted to combine my interest in still life with my experiences working in Media Technology Services. I often hear that even with thousands of dollars of presentation technology in each classroom, the overall sentiment towards the tech is neutral or negative. My photographs tried to present it in a way that people on BC's campus haven't seen before, adding a colorful, and hopefully postive twist on technology that is seen as purely a utilitarian tool.
