Reuben Allik is a senior at Boston College majoring in Economics, with double minors in Computer Science and Marketing, and is currently seeking employment in an Analytics Role.

For his final undergraduate semester, Reuben is conducting Natural Lanaguage Processing research on review-based, online platforms and the detection of fake reviews. Professionally, Reuben is working in both Classroom Technology Support, under BC's Media Technology Services department, and for Professor Linda Salisbury in CSOM's Marketing Department as a Teaching Assistant (Marketing Analytics and Customer Research). He also contributes as a Research Assistant, helping with UX/UI design for consumer research survey material and with data cleaning.

Outside of the professional and academic relm, Reuben is a close-defenseman on the BC Men's Club Lacrosse team, a member of the BC Hillel Executive board, and a fellow with the Miller Center's Boston Interfaith Leadership Initiative. Reuben curates the social media pages for both BC organizations and manages film operations for the lacrosse team.
