Photos from the Whitney Plantation, Edgard, Louisiana

My afternoon tour of the Whitney Plantation was a powerful oportunity to learn more about a truely evil institution and a foundational aspect of American History. Our tour guide's description of the psycology that is put into dividing a population will remain with me for the rest of my life. The imagery of Woodrow Nash's Children of Whitney scupltures will always remind me that power imbalances and oppression need to be activly fought against, being passive will always let them back through the cracks.
